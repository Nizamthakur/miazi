Shipping Options

We offer various shipping options to meet your needs:

  • Inside-Dhaka Shipping: Delivery within 1-3 Working days. Standard delivery charge is 70 Taka
  • Outside-Dhaka Shipping: Delivery within 2-5 Working days. Standard delivery charge is 110 Taka
  • Express-Shipping (For Inside Dhaka Only): Same-day delivery for urgent orders.

Tracking Your Order

To track your order please follow the following steps.
Step 1. Visit www.miabaaba.com
Step 2. Go to the track my order.
Step 3. Paste your tracking id. See your invoice for the tracking Id Example "3DNU3FG"
